Here are 5 clear rules for agents handling card linking issues:

- **Verify Card Status**: Before providing instructions, verify that the customer's card is not already linked to their account and that it has not been previously cancelled or reported lost/stolen.

- **Provide App-Based Instructions**: Inform customers that they can link their card to their account through the mobile app, and provide step-by-step instructions on how to do so, including navigating to the "Add Card" or "Link Card" section.

- **Explain Card Activation**: Clearly explain to customers that they need to activate their card before it can be linked to their account, and provide instructions on how to activate it, if necessary.

- **Handle Re-Activation of Previously Cancelled Cards**: If a customer reports finding a previously cancelled card, inform them that they need to request re-activation of the card through the call center, and provide instructions on how to do so.

- **Escalate Complex Issues**: If a customer is experiencing technical issues or difficulties linking their card through the app, offer to escalate the issue to a technical support specialist or a senior agent for further assistance.
